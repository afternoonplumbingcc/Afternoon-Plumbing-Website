Frost-Free Hose Bibs: Why They Matter in Maryland

Standard outdoor faucets have the shut-off valve right behind the handle, exposing it to freezing temperatures. Frost-free (also called “freeze-proof”) hose bibs place the shut-off deep inside the home, where it stays warm. When you close the valve and disconnect the hose, any residual water drains out, leaving an empty pipe that can't freeze and burst.

For Westminster winters, a frost-free upgrade is a smart, long-term solution that prevents expensive burst-pipe repairs and water damage.
