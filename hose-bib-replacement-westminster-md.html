When replacement makes sense

Common reasons to replace instead of repair

  • The faucet keeps leaking and has already been patched before
  • Freeze damage has affected the body or shutoff area
  • A frost-free upgrade is the smarter long-term choice
  • The exterior finish or connection looks rough and needs a cleaner reset
What frost-free means

The shutoff point sits farther back inside the wall.

That design helps reduce freeze-related damage compared with older styles that shut off closer to the exterior handle.
