Safety Warning

Water Heater Leaking Into the Electrical Area With Orange Deposits

Location: Westminster, Maryland 21157

This Westminster service call shows an active leak reaching the electrical section of the water heater, with clear orange mineral/rust deposits around upper components. Both images on this page are before photos from the same job. When moisture reaches wiring and element terminals, the risk goes beyond a normal plumbing leak.

In conditions like this, continuing to run the unit can accelerate corrosion, damage components, and create unsafe operating conditions. In most cases, the safest path is immediate shutdown, professional diagnosis, and replacement planning rather than piecemeal repair on a compromised tank.

Critical Signs Homeowners Should Not Ignore

  • Moisture near electrical access panels
  • Orange or rust-colored deposits at the top element area
  • Rust streaking down the tank shell
  • Intermittent hot water and rising electric usage
  • Any burning smell, buzzing, or tripped breaker
