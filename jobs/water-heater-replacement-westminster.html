Emergency Water Heater Replacement in Westminster, MD 21157

Location: Westminster, Maryland 21157

We were called after the homeowner noticed rusting around the shell and active water leakage from the top heating element on an electric 50-gallon water heater. With visible deterioration and ongoing leak risk, we provided replacement options and recommended a long-term upgrade.

The homeowner selected a BG&E rebate-eligible Rheem 50-gallon heat pump water heater. We completed the replacement in about four hours, tested the full system, and left the home with reliable hot water and improved efficiency.
