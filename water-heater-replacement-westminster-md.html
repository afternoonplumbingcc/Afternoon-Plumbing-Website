Water heater FAQ
Questions

Water heater FAQ highlights

How do I know whether repair still makes sense?

If the heater is newer and the tank is not leaking, repair may still be reasonable. A leaking tank usually points toward replacement.

Can you replace a leaking heater the same day?

Sometimes, yes, depending on availability and the equipment needed for the job.

What should I do if the heater is actively leaking?

Shut off the cold-water feed if you can. If it is electric, shut it off at the panel. If it is gas, turn off the gas valve next to the heater, then call.
