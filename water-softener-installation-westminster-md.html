Questions

Water treatment FAQ highlights

How do I know what size system I need?

Capacity depends on family size, water usage, and the actual hardness level. The water test helps determine the right fit.

Can a water softener remove iron too?

A standard softener can handle small amounts of dissolved iron, but visible staining often points to additional treatment needs.

What if I also have acidic water?

Some homes need an acid neutralizer plus a softener. That recommendation depends on test results and the condition of the plumbing.
